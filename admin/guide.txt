**Developer Notes**

Order Status
0 - Pending / In Progress
1 - Completed
2 - Cancelled

Payment Status
0 - Pending
1 - Paid

=> For orders with online payment, the payment status will be 1. 
=> For Cash On Delivery (COD), initially the payment status will be 0. Once the order is delivered and
cash is received from the customer, you can update the order status to completed and the payment status
will automatically be updated as 1 (paid).
